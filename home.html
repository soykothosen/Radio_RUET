About Radio RUET
Radio RUET is the newly member of online bangla radio community. Its started their journey from May 25, 2017. Its streaming from the Heart of RUET (Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology) Powered by Pothik. From the very first they started with RJ program.

Radio RUET trying to broadcast quality music and Rj program. Their RJ’s always try to maintain the quality of their program. Best wishes for them from OnlineBanglaRadio. You can chat with the RJ’s of the station through the Facebook Chat option from the right side of our website.
